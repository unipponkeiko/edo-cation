Program

Unique Elegance

When we create a gathering, what matters most is uniqueness and refinement. We bring together the arts of Nihon Buyo classical dance, Ukiyo-e woodblock printing, tea ceremony, and Rakugo storytelling — weaving them into singular, once-in-a-lifetime experiences that gently guide the spirit toward the essence of Japanese culture.

山本　桂子　Keiko Yamamoto

Cultural Relationship Developer

A graduate of Waseda University, Keiko began studying Nihon Buyo classical Japanese dance at age eight under Wakana Isanami, and later trained under the fourth Iemoto of the Wakana school, Wakana Isaburo. She obtained her teaching licence in 2018 and leads a dance studio in Tokyo's Taito ward.

Drawing on the aesthetic sensibility and deep knowledge of Japanese culture cultivated through dance, she produces and coordinates cultural events and workshops for international audiences.

Member, Japan Dance Association
